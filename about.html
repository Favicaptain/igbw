Our Story

History, Mission, and Leadership of the Diocese

Our History

Founded in 1892, the Diocese of Igbomina West has been a cornerstone of the community for over a century. What began as a small gathering of faithful believers has grown into a diverse and active diocese covering twelve counties.

We continue to build on the legacy of our founders, adapting to the needs of the modern world while remaining rooted in the timeless truths of the Gospel.

Our Leadership

The Right Rev. John Smith

Bishop of St. Jude

Bishop John was consecrated in 2015 and has since led the diocese with a focus on youth outreach and social justice. He holds a Doctorate in Divinity from Oxford and is passionate about environmental stewardship.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To proclaim the Good News of the Kingdom, teach, baptize, and nurture new believers.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of light and hope, transforming lives through the love of Christ.
